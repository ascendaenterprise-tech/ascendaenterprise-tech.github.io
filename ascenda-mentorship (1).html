Questions

Common questions

Do I need prior trading experience?
+
No — but you need the right mindset. Beginners with serious intent are welcome. What matters is your commitment, your coachability, and your willingness to do the work. Experience helps, but hunger matters more.
Why is pricing not listed publicly?
+
Because pricing is not the filter — fit is. We don't want people applying based on a number. We want to understand who you are first, then discuss investment. Every applicant is treated individually.
How many spots are available?
+
Extremely few. This is intentional. Izaiah only takes on mentees he can give real time and attention to. When spots are full, the program closes — sometimes for months. Apply now if you're serious.
What happens after I apply?
+
Your application is reviewed personally by Izaiah. If you're a potential fit, you'll be contacted for a direct conversation to discuss your goals, your situation, and which tier makes sense. Most applications are declined — but every one is read.
Is there a refund policy?
+
All sales are final. This is a commitment — from you and from Izaiah. We take this seriously and expect you to as well. We only accept mentees we genuinely believe we can help, which is why the application process exists.
Can I upgrade tiers mid-program?
+
Yes, on a case-by-case basis. If you're in the Foundation or Accelerator tier and want to move up, we can discuss upgrading at the appropriate time. This is always at Izaiah's discretion.
