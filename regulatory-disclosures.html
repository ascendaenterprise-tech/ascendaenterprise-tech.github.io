4. Accredited Investor Requirement

Any investment opportunity offered by Ascenda Enterprise Capital LLC is available exclusively to Accredited Investors as defined under Rule 501 of Regulation D of the Securities Act of 1933, or Qualified Institutional Buyers as defined under Rule 144A of the Securities Act of 1933.

Under current SEC regulations, an individual qualifies as an Accredited Investor if they:

  • Have a net worth exceeding $1,000,000, excluding the value of their primary residence, either individually or jointly with a spouse or spousal equivalent; OR
  • Have income exceeding $200,000 in each of the two most recent years (or $300,000 jointly with a spouse or spousal equivalent) with a reasonable expectation of the same in the current year; OR
  • Hold in good standing a Series 7, 65, or 82 securities license; OR
  • Are a "knowledgeable employee" of a private fund

By submitting any application or investment inquiry to AEC, you represent and warrant that you meet the applicable accredited investor definition and agree to provide verification documentation upon request.
