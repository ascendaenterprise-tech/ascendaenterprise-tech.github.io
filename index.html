Investment strategy

The AEC Method

Three proprietary frameworks operating in concert — engineered to identify high-probability entries, eliminate noise, and execute with institutional precision.

Layer I — Momentum Engine

Advanced Momentum

Multi-timeframe momentum analysis identifies the direction and strength of institutional order flow before entries are considered. We trade with the current, never against it. Trend confirmation across three timeframes is mandatory before any position is initiated.

Layer II — Statistical Edge

Z-Score Standard Deviation

Proprietary Z-score analysis quantifies price deviation from statistical mean, identifying when assets are extended beyond sustainable levels. This framework eliminates emotional decision-making, replacing it with mathematical precision and a defined statistical edge on every trade.

Layer III — Institutional Flow

DOM & Order Flow

Depth of Market and advanced order flow analysis reveals what institutions are actually doing — not what price suggests they're doing. By reading the tape at the microstructure level, AEC identifies the precise moments when institutional capital enters and exits the market.
