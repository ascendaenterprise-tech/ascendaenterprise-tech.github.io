Investment strategy

The AEC Method

Three proprietary frameworks operating in concert — engineered to identify high-probability entries, eliminate noise, and execute with institutional precision.

Layer I — Momentum Engine

Advanced Momentum

Multi-timeframe momentum analysis identifies the direction and strength of institutional order flow before entries are considered. We trade with the current, never against it. Trend confirmation across three timeframes is mandatory before any position is initiated.

Layer II — Statistical Edge

Z-Score Standard Deviation

Proprietary Z-score analysis quantifies price deviation from statistical mean, identifying when assets are extended beyond sustainable levels. This framework eliminates emotional decision-making, replacing it with mathematical precision and a defined statistical edge on every trade.

Layer III — Institutional Flow

DOM & Order Flow

Depth of Market and advanced order flow analysis reveals what institutions are actually doing — not what price suggests they're doing. By reading the tape at the microstructure level, AEC identifies the precise moments when institutional capital enters and exits the market.

Results may vary. Past strategy performance is not indicative of future results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can invest with Ascenda Enterprise Capital?
+
AEC is open exclusively to Accredited Investors as defined by the SEC — individuals with a net worth exceeding $1,000,000 (excluding primary residence) or annual income exceeding $200,000 individually ($300,000 jointly). Institutional allocators and qualified family offices are also welcome to inquire.
What is the minimum investment?
+
Minimum investment starts at $50,000 for private investors. Institutional allocators and family offices typically deploy $100,000 and above. All investment amounts are subject to review and approval by AEC leadership.
What is the Quantum Trading System?
+
AEC's Quantum Trading System is a proprietary, internally-developed trading framework that combines advanced momentum analysis, Z-score standard deviation modeling, and institutional DOM order flow reading. It is not licensed, not sold, and not available outside of AEC. It exists exclusively to generate returns for our clients and our firm.
What markets does AEC trade?
+
AEC's primary trading instruments include NAS100, ES Futures, Gold, Crude Oil, and the top-performing equities of the decade including NVDA, TSLA, AMZN, AAPL, MSFT, and META. We operate across both long and short positions using systematic momentum and order flow strategies.
How do I know if I qualify as an Accredited Investor?
+
Under SEC Regulation D Rule 501, you qualify as an Accredited Investor if you have a net worth exceeding $1,000,000 excluding your primary residence, OR annual income exceeding $200,000 individually (or $300,000 jointly with a spouse) in each of the last two years with expectation of the same this year. Holders of Series 7, 65, or 82 licenses also qualify automatically.
What happens after I submit an inquiry?
+
Every inquiry is reviewed personally by Izaiah Cottle within 24–48 hours. If there is a potential fit, you will receive a direct response to schedule an introductory call. AEC is selective about who we work with — quality over quantity, always.
Is AEC registered with the SEC?
+
Ascenda Enterprise Capital LLC is a registered Limited Liability Company in the State of Georgia. AEC is not currently registered as an Investment Adviser with the SEC or any state authority. All investment activities are conducted in compliance with applicable federal and state securities laws.
